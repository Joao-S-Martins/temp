About João Martins John Martins

I'm a software engineer working around the San Francisco Bay. Some might call me full-stack, but I prefer front-focused. I've been with JavaScript since back when folks thought it was a joke, and have added Java, perl, PHP, and python as-needed.

As much as I'd like to say that this site exists to better the sofware community, it really doesn't. It's just about me; my experiences, lessons, and thoughts to be shared beyond what my physical being is capable of.
